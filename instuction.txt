You may need to modify these steps slightly if you're not using 1080p.

1. Launch the program
2. Zoom out (Ctrl + Mousedown) Exactly 11 times (on some mice some scroll clicks are not registered, make sure you actually zoom in).
11 times)
3. Maximize the window
4. Press f11
5. Press enter
6. Enlarge to desired size
7. Press Enter again

The trap is now set and the video will play when you press the enter button.
